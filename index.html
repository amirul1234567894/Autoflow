Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find answers below — or ask us directly on Telegram.

Do I need to know how to code? ▼
Not at all! We build the entire system for you. You just tell us what you want automated — we handle everything else. Zero technical knowledge required.
Do you really deliver in 24 hours? ▼
Yes! Most projects are delivered within 12–24 hours. Complex projects may take up to 48 hours. We keep you updated at every step via Telegram.
What is n8n? How does it work? ▼
n8n is a powerful open-source automation tool — like Zapier or Make, but more powerful and affordable. We install it on your server, build your custom workflows, and connect your Telegram bot.
Is it a one-time payment or recurring? ▼
It's a one-time setup fee. No hidden monthly charges from us. Your automation runs for life. There may be a small server hosting cost (usually ₹200–500/month) which you pay directly to the hosting provider.
Is my data safe and secure? ▼
100% safe. Your data lives on your own server — we never share it with any third party. Telegram is end-to-end encrypted. n8n is self-hosted, meaning you have complete control over everything.
How do I get support after setup? ▼
We're available directly on Telegram. Starter = 2 weeks support, Business = 2 months, Full Stack = 6 months priority support. You're also added to our exclusive community for ongoing help and updates.
